Pumpkin pie

Description

The beautiful pie is made by adding incredible ingredient into a boil

Some put the tv on channel 3 to help it work

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces semisweet chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1 unbaked pie crust
  • 1 pumpkin puree
  • 1 sweetened condensed milk
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C) & fit pie crust on a baking sheet.
  2. Melt the chocolate in the microwave for 30-second intervals, stirring between intervals
  3. Combine pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, and pumpkin pie spice
  4. Pour the pie filling into the pie shell. Marbled effect by swirling in and out of the dollops.
  5. Bake in for 35 to 40 minutes. Remove from the oven.
